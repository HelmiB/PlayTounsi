# Delete Your Play Tounsi Account

If you would like to delete your **Play Tounsi** account and associated data, follow the steps below.

## How to Request Account Deletion

Send an email to **playtounsi.app@gmail.com** with:

- **Subject:** Account Deletion Request
- **Body:** Your registered email address (the one used to sign in with Google or Facebook)

We will process your request within **7 days**.

## What Gets Deleted

| Data | Action |
|---|---|
| Account profile (name, email, profile picture) | Permanently deleted |
| Game scores and progress | Permanently deleted |
| Firebase authentication record | Permanently deleted |

## What May Be Retained

Anonymous, aggregated statistics (e.g. total games played with no link to your identity) may be retained for up to **30 days** for internal analytics, after which they are permanently deleted.

## Questions?

Contact us at **playtounsi.app@gmail.com**
